Star Defense — Privacy Policy

Effective 21 July 2026 · Applies to the Star Defense website (star-defense.allyourbase3.workers.dev) and the Star Defense Android app (com.allyourbase3.stardefense).

The short version

Star Defense has no accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind. The only thing that ever leaves your device is an optional leaderboard entry — a display name you choose and your score — and only if you tap Save.

Leaderboard

When you finish a run with a high enough score, you may enter a display name and submit it to a shared leaderboard. If you do, the name you type, your score, the level you reached, and whether you cleared it are stored in our database and shown publicly to other players. This is entirely optional — if you never tap Save, nothing is submitted. Please don't put personal information in the name field, since it becomes public. Choose any nickname you like. To have an entry removed, email us below.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress (maps beaten, best waves, checkpoints, and sound settings) is saved locally on your own device using your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device and we cannot see it. You can erase it at any time by clearing the site's browsing data (or the app's storage on Android).

Server logs

The game is served by Cloudflare. Like virtually all web hosting, Cloudflare may briefly process technical information such as IP addresses to deliver the site and protect it from abuse. We do not use this information to identify anyone, and we run no analytics on it.

Children

Because the game collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes

If the game ever adds features that handle additional personal data, this policy will be updated first and the effective date above will change.
