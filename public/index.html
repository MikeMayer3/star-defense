ABOUT

Star Defense is a neon tower-defense game. Alien fleets fly a glowing lane toward your mothership across 40 levels of 30 waves each.

Pick a turret from the shop, drop it on an open tile, and press START WAVE. Kills earn Space Bucks to buy and upgrade turrets — and you'll need those upgrades, because the enemies never stop scaling. Lose all 20 shields and the fleet breaks through.
